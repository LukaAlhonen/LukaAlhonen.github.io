About Me

Hello!

My name is Luka and I am a 22-year-old third year computer engineering student at Åbo Akademi University from Turku, Finland. Computer science and software development have been interests of mine for quite some time. I had my first introduction to programming at the age of 16, with the python programming language and soon after I started using the Linux operating system as my daily driver.

During my studies and free time I have matured my programming skills by learning more about data structures, algorithms, security, web technologies, design patterns and more.

As a worker I have been told by Employers and Officers in the army that I am very diligent and able to learn from and receive feedback and constructive criticism very well.

Education

I graduated from Borgå Gymnasium upper secondary school in 2019 and started my compulsory military service, during which I received leadership training as an NCO. In the autumn of 2020 I began my computer engineering studies at Åbo Akademi and I am currently finishing up my bachelors degree, which I hope to receive by the end of spring 2023.

During my studies I have completed courses in software development, algorithms, databases, web technologies, computer networks and security, computer architecture, systems and control engineering, physics and mathematics.

Skills

  • Java, JavaScript(React.js, Node.js), Python(Django), C/C++, SQL
  • Linux/Windows
  • Algorithm analysis, data structures, Object Oriented programming, Fullstack development, databases(mysql, sqlite, mongodb)
  • Git/Github
